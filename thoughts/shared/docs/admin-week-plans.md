# Week Plans (Admin) (/admin/week-plans)

## Purpose
Assemble a full 7-day schedule by assigning a Day Plan to each weekday.

## Audience
- Admins: Full access.
- Users: Not available; non-admins are redirected to the Dashboard.

## How to use (step-by-step)
1. Open Week Plans from the Admin navigation.
2. Click New Week Plan.
3. Enter code, name, and optional description.
4. Assign a Day Plan to each day of the week (all 7 required).
5. Review the weekly summary (work days and total hours) and save.
6. Use View, Edit, Copy, or Delete from the data table.

## Data and fields
- Code, name, description.
- Day Plan assignment for each weekday.
- Active status.

## Where changes take effect / integrations
- Tariffs use Week Plans for weekly or rolling-weekly rhythms.
- Employees inherit day schedules via tariffs and employee day plans.

## Troubleshooting
- All seven days must have a Day Plan assigned.
- If the Day Plan dropdown is empty, create Day Plans first.
